[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can hear it falling outside the door
Tracing every drop upon the floor
Your shadow dances in the candle glow
[Whispered] (Moving sweet and pulling slow)

[Verse 1]
A rusted porch swing and a midnight breeze
Silver moonlight tangling in the trees
You look at me with fire in your stare
Leaving memories we are bound to share

[Pre-Chorus]
The thunder rolls across the canyon line
I pour a glass of honeyed summertime
You lean in close to catch a gentle breath
Sparking embers right inside my chest

[Chorus]
Whispering names in the pouring rain
Washing away the dust and the pain
Two wild hearts running out of the cold
A midnight story waiting to be told

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Running out of the cold)
A midnight story waiting to be told

[Verse 2]

Time is heavy and it stands so still
